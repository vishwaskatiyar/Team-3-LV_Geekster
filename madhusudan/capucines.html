The Capucines

An homage to Louis Vuitton’s first store, opened in 1854 on Rue Neuve-des-Capucines in Paris, the Capucines exemplifies the artisanal savoir-faire of this historic area. Unveiled in an array of colour and sizes, the model, worn in multiple ways, epitomises timeless elegance.

Exclusively available in Louis Vuitton stores.

Savoir-Faire

Transcending the limits of leather goods, the Capucines is a totem of ingenuity and unparalleled savoir-faire. Behind the apparent simplicity of its design lies a complex creative process and extensive skill: each bag is assembled in 250 steps by the Maison’s most experienced artisans.

Artycapucines

The Capucines’ timeless silhouette provides an ideal emblem for the artistic Artycapucines collaboration: an invitation for celebrated artists to imbue the iconic model with their creative visions as a testament to Louis Vuitton’s pursuit of innovation and excellence.
